# DMCA Takedown Notice 模板

> **用途**：当发现第三方未经授权使用 PromiseLink 代码（违反 AGPL v3）时，使用本模板向托管平台发送下架通知。
>
> **使用前提**：仅在确认对方确实违反 AGPL v3 许可证后使用。建议先尝试友好沟通。

## DMCA Takedown Notice Template

```text
Subject: DMCA Takedown Notice - Copyright Infringement

Dear [Platform Name] Copyright Agent,

I, the undersigned, hereby submit this Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) takedown notice pursuant to 17 U.S.C. § 512(c).

### 1. Copyright Owner Information

Name: [你的姓名/公司名]
Address: [你的地址]
Email: [你的邮箱]
Phone: [你的电话]

### 2. Identification of Copyrighted Work

The copyrighted work is the source code of "PromiseLink", an AI-driven personal business relationship management application, licensed under GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0).

- Original Repository: https://github.com/lulin70/PromiseLink
- License: AGPL-3.0 (https://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl-3.0.html)
- Copyright Notice: Copyright (c) 2026 PromiseLink Team

### 3. Identification of Infringing Material

The following material infringes our copyright by using PromiseLink source code without complying with AGPL v3 license terms:

- Infringing URL: [侵权仓库/网站URL]
- Infringing Content: [具体描述侵权内容]
- Violation Type: [选择: 未保留版权声明 / 未开源修改后的代码 / 移除品牌标识 / 闭源商业使用]

### 4. Good Faith Belief Statement

I have a good faith belief that the use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

### 5. Accuracy Statement

I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

### 6. Electronic Signature

[你的电子签名]
Date: [日期]

Sincerely,
[你的姓名]
[你的职务]
PromiseLink Team
```

## 使用流程

1. **确认侵权**：检查对方是否确实使用了 PromiseLink 代码且违反 AGPL v3
2. **友好沟通**（可选）：先尝试联系对方，要求合规
3. **发送通知**：填写本模板，发送至对应平台
4. **跟踪处理**：平台通常在 1-3 个工作日内响应
5. **记录归档**：将所有通信记录归档至 `docs/legal/cases/` 目录

## AGPL v3 关键条款参考

- **§5**：修改后的作品必须以 AGPL v3 许可发布
- **§7**：不得施加额外限制
- **§13**：网络服务提供者也必须开源修改（Remote Network Interaction）

## 注意事项

- DMCA 通知必须基于真实侵权，虚假通知可能导致法律责任
- 建议在发送前咨询法律顾问
- 保留所有证据截图和通信记录
